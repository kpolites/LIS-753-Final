Women in Art History

For millenia, female painters have been overlooked or, in some cases, their work has been claimed by men. Now, as feminist art movements are gaining ground and popularity, the truth about female painters in history is being discovered while a new generation of women promote past, current, and personal art.

This page will direct you to three famous women in art history: Artemisia Gentileschi, Frida Kahlo, and Georgia O'Keeffe. You will also find another link that covers 10 of the most important female painters who have influenced art history.
