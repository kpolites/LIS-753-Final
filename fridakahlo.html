Frida Kahlo

Frida Kahlo is arguably the most famous female painter of all time. Her bold colors, social commentary, and self-portraits are among her most well-known artistic attributes. Her life was plagued by several physical tragedies and a tumultuous marriage to Diego Rivera; however, she found her voice through art. She is celebrated in her native Mexico for drawing attention to Mexican and indiginous culture and by feminists for her depiction of the female experience and form.
